# General Ledger

## Overview

General Ledger is the core financial module in Dynamics 365 Finance & Operations.

---

## Setup Steps

1. Create Fiscal Calendar
2. Create Ledger
3. Configure Chart of Accounts
4. Configure Financial Dimensions
5. Configure Posting Profiles

---

## Business Scenario

ABC Company wants to maintain financial transactions across multiple legal entities while sharing a common chart of accounts.

---

## Best Practices

- Use Financial Dimensions effectively.
- Keep the Chart of Accounts simple.
- Test posting profiles before production.
